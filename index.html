NEW MAP - SOLARA

Solara - The new tropical paradise map

Begin a new adventure in this vibrant city!

Welcome to Solara, a lively summer port city with beautiful Jacaranda trees and charming tropical scenery. This map features stunning dual peak points and an exciting slide rail system, offering diverse combat strategies and exploration opportunities. Whether you're navigating through flower-filled streets or enjoying romantic moments under the Ferris wheel, Solara offers endless possibilities!

The Solara map combines realistic and subtly sci-fi art styles with a color palette of blues, whites, and bright yellows, inspired by seaside port cities. It features distinctive landscapes like valleys, waterfalls, and Jacaranda trees, along with several unique locations that each offer unique experiences:

  • Studio: A film set simulation area with distinctive backlot buildings. Two steam trains run toward each other in the center, creating special strategic opportunities for combat in this zone.
  • Cascadale: A perfectly symmetrical research center with reflective slides as key structural features. Use the slides to quickly position yourself for combat advantage.
  • Bloomtown: The heart of the city features winding flower streets filled with blossoms. Originally a steep and dangerous straight road, it has been redesigned into S-shaped curves decorated with beautiful Jacaranda trees.
  • Sewage Works: A sewage treatment plant with yellow pipes running through it. These pipes contain secret paths for exploration, but beware of enemies hiding inside.

The entire Solara map is filled with slide rails and stations, allowing players to quickly move around the map. While on a rail, players can deploy special Gloo Walls that surround them for protection. If there are enemies nearby, the rail will flash red as a warning.

The new map will be released in both BR-Ranked and CS-Ranked modes. During the first week, players will not lose rank points after their first daily loss in BR-Ranked or stars after their first daily loss in CS-Ranked.

During the event, CS matches on Solara will consist of two consecutive rounds in the same area of the map after the first round, and teams will switch spawn points between rounds.

BATTLE ROYALE

8TH ANNIVERSARY

On this anniversary, let's board the Infinity Train together.

"The celebration all Survivors have been waiting for is here! The Infinity Train bound for the 8th Anniversary is about to depart, traveling across all maps, inviting all Survivors who dare to board it. This is more than just an adventure: it's a formal invitation to the Infinity Arena! Compete for exclusive Infinity Items and push your limits!"

  • Players will spawn in the Infinite Arena instead of the plane's cockpit. A train will circle the Arena: be careful, it might knock you down!
  • On all maps, 8 Infinite Stations will open in 4 stages, with 2 unlockable stations in each stage.
  • After a station opens, the Infinity Train will arrive at a certain time, start a 30-second departure countdown, and trigger one of four special events:
    • Gloo Wall Recovery Zone: Within a certain range, Gloo Makers will generate Gloo Walls at 300% of their normal speed.
    • Boosters: Within a certain range, a large number of stackable speed, damage, and shield boosters will appear.
    • Self-Revive Zone: Within a certain range, players will be given a chance to revive without assistance.
    • Special Airdrops: Within a certain range, multiple special Airdrops containing Infinite Weapons will drop.
  • When the departure countdown ends, the team with the most players inside the station will be able to enter the Infinite Arena. Teammates within a 50m radius will also be allowed to enter, and any downed or eliminated teammates will be revived upon entering the Arena.
  • The two teams in the Arena will battle it out to determine the winner. The losing team will still receive 8th Anniversary rewards and parachute back into the regular match, while the winning team will earn one of four special Infinite items:
    • Infinite Gloo Maker: After using all of your Gloo Walls, you'll be able to deploy unlimited Gloo Walls for 15 seconds. After that, the number of Gloo Walls you have will be fully reset.
    • Infinite Inhaler: Heals 10 HP every 2 seconds and restores 10 XP per second.
    • Infinite Resurrection Card: Allows you to revive once with all the equipment you had.
    • Infinity Chip: Upgrades all your weapons to Infinite Weapons during the match, increasing their damage by 15%.

Infinity Train

  • When the game reaches round 3, a special train will activate one of the following three effects on the game:
    • Boosted Express: Drops movement speed, healing, and weapon damage boosts on the ground.

    • Armed Express: Drops special 8th Anniversary airdrops containing exclusive weapons for players to capture.

CHARACTERS

Character balance changes in OB49 update

CHARACTER IMPROVEMENTS

Paloma

New scope for the master of firepower

With recent changes increasing backpack capacity allowing players to carry more ammo, Paloma's skill of extra ammo storage has become less significant. The new Paloma enhances weapon accuracy while pressuring enemies and penetrating ice walls, allowing quick elimination of enemies hiding behind ice walls.

Scatter: Weapon accuracy increases for 10 seconds after destroying an ice wall

CHARACTER BALANCE

Ignis

Double the firepower!

With Ignis' adjustments now live, his new flaming scene has caused a stir in Bermuda. However, its effect from a single use still doesn't compare to other skills. This time we've doubled the power to enhance performance. Hopefully, Ignis will truly shine on the battlefield.

Maya Flame: Creates a wide 10m fire wall lasting 10 seconds and moving forward at 6m/s for up to 5s → 8s. While active, use skill again to stop the wall. Enemies (or ice walls) touching it take immediate damage of 25 (or 400) and will take 5 (or 100 → 150) damage per second for 2s. Cooldown: 60s. Can store up to 2 skill uses with 0.5s cooldown between uses.

Layla

One more ice wall!

In Bermuda, ice walls are essential for survival, especially in long-range combat where obtaining ice walls often depends on luck. With the new patch, Layla changes the game by doubling ice wall rewards from her skill, ensuring long-range fighters always have enough. Thanks to Layla, ice walls are no longer a special luxury item. Players can use ice walls freely and strategically anytime, anywhere.

Deadly Ice: When attacking enemies or vehicles with rifles and sniper rifles, reduces enemy movement speed by 10% or vehicle acceleration by 80% for 3s. If enemies are downed while slowed, they can be frozen for up to 3s. Receive additional ice walls: 1 → 2.

Luqueta

Adjusted power curve with early and late game strength

Luqueta's abilities often encourage players to engage in combat for more advantages. While the maximum stack limit for his skill effects has increased to 3, the shield values from the first two stacks have been slightly reduced to balance this change.

Hat-trick: Each elimination permanently increases max shield by 20 → 15. Maximum increase: 40 → 45. Effects clear when player is eliminated or new round begins.

Moco

The all-seeing eye of the mighty Enigma

Moco's ability to mark enemy positions makes her both an excellent scout and strong teammate, synergizing well with characters like Mako for heavy damage and Hayato for extra coins. These powerful combos and superior intel-gathering abilities make her a standout force. For balance, we've slightly reduced Moco's enemy marking duration. After all, no one stays on top forever.

Enigma's Eye: Marks attacked enemies, revealing their position on battlefield view, minimap, and sharing with teammates for 5s → 4s. Enemies moving while marked have their position revealed longer, up to 6.5s → 5.5s.

Luna

Powerful movement capabilities

With her unique combination of increased fire rate and special movement speed, Luna has become a force in close combat, controlling the flow of battle. In the next update, we'll adjust her mobility while maintaining her signature rapid-fire style. Luna players will need to be more careful with positioning to maximize effectiveness on the battlefield.

Custom Bullets: Increases fire rate by 8% when user attacks enemies. Maximum fire rate of 15% → 10% is converted to movement speed, and changes reset when user survives combat.

WEAPONS & BALANCE

Weapon balance changes in OB49 update

WEAPON BALANCE

Assault Rifles: SCAR, M4A1, M14, FAMAS, AUG, GROZA, PLASMA

In recent patches, the overall strength of non-upgradable rifles has increased, making early-game combat smoother but narrowing the gap between high-level and non-upgradable rifles. To enhance mid-to-late game weapon experience, the new patch will increase the power and ice wall penetration of high-level and upgradable rifles. Additionally, PLASMA's ice wall penetration will receive a slight buff. Furthermore, the M14 will be reclassified from rifle to sniper rifle with increased long-range power.

  • M14/M14-I/M14-II/M14-III: Reclassified as sniper rifle. Armor penetration +10%, damage to ice walls +30%
  • SCAR/SCAR-I/SCAR-II/SCAR-III: Damage +3%, damage to ice walls +30%
  • M4A1/M4A1-I/M4A1-II/M4A1-III: Damage +5%, damage to ice walls +30%
  • GROZA/GROZA-X: Damage to ice walls +30%
  • FAMAS/FAMAS-I/FAMAS-II/FAMAS-III: Damage to ice walls +20%
  • AUG/AUG-I/AUG-II/AUG-III: Damage to ice walls +20%
  • PLASMA (Buff): Damage to ice walls +50%

Machine Guns: M60 and M249

Machine guns have been forgotten for too long, but now M60 and M249 are getting enhanced in both damage and mobility.

  • M60/M60-I/M60-II/M60-III: Ammo capacity 60 → 70, armor penetration +8%, movement speed while firing +20%
  • M249/M249-X: Armor penetration +8%, movement speed while firing +10%

Healing Gun

The healing gun is one of the few tools for restoring HP that can be used, but its effectiveness is often limited by the challenge of healing teammates while moving. In this update, we've improved its accuracy, making it easier for players to support their team in the heat of battle.

Healing Gun: Accuracy +20%

Trogon (Grenade Launcher Mode)

After adjustments regarding explosions in previous patches, Trogon's grenade launcher mode has fallen out of favor with players. To address this, we're slightly increasing the power and range of this mode.

Trogon (Grenade Launcher Mode): Damage +15%, range +10%

ARMOR ATTACHMENT IMPROVEMENTS

Some armor attachments have seen relatively low usage in matches despite playing important roles in combat. This makes it difficult for players to estimate enemy HP and damage during fights. In the new update, all armor attachments will be adjusted to have clearer roles in combat.

  • Steel Helmet Plate: Headshot damage -35% → -10%, helmet durability +10%
  • HP Armor: Shield and max shield +30 → +20
  • Armor Extender: Limb damage reduction: 25%, shield and max shield +30 → Limb damage reduction: 10%, shield and max shield +20

NEW WEAPON IMPROVEMENTS

Kord

Kord has been unpopular for a long time and was once known for its ice wall penetration but lacked standout combat power. After the remake, it combines strong combat capabilities with effective ice wall penetration, performing well both when firing standing or crouching.

  • Standing: Fires three low-power rounds at a rapid rate
  • Crouching: Fires one high-power round at a moderate rate

VSS

As one of the most unique weapons in the game, VSS has several special effects that have caused both favor and frustration among players. However, its presence in matches has declined recently, so we're improving VSS to make it a powerful weapon once again!

  • Removed bleeding effect and upgrade mechanism
  • Added 3-round burst auto-fire mode, significantly increasing fire rate, accuracy, and magazine capacity

Healing Gun-Y

The Healing Gun-Y is a high-power weapon for assisting teammates, but its complex mechanism has made many unable to fully utilize its rescue function. In the new patch, this special mechanism has been simplified. Players can rescue downed teammates simply by shooting them with the Healing Gun-Y!

  • Heals living teammates for 25 HP by shooting; revives downed teammates by shooting (no effect on Tiva)
  • No limit on rescues

OTHER IMPROVEMENTS

  • Bullet traces from off-screen enemies are more visible, making it easier to identify shots from high or hard-to-cover positions
  • Attacks that penetrate ice walls (e.g., M82B, Iris' skill) will have clearer visual effects when attacking ice walls

WEAPON SOUND IMPROVEMENTS

Clearer combat feedback sounds

In the past, the lack of clear sound feedback when attacking different types of objects created unnecessary complexity in intense combat situations. To address this, we've added new combat sound effects in the latest patch. These changes not only make feedback clearer but also enhance your situational awareness in combat.

  • New sound effects when attacking:
    • Ice walls
    • Armor
    • Bodies
    • Destructible crates
  • Improved sound effects when attacking vehicles

NEW PRIME SYSTEM

Become Prime and unlock exclusive rewards!

"It's been an incredible journey, and we couldn't have done it without your passion and support! We're excited to announce the new Prime System, designed to reward each player with unique privileges based on their top-up history."

  • About Prime: The Prime System will launch on June 1st. Players only need to earn 100 Diamonds to join. There are 8 Prime Tiers, each offering exclusive privileges, with higher tiers including all the privileges of the lower tiers. Prime privileges include special status tokens such as:
    • Prime Insignia (Badges)
    • Prime Icon and Frame
    • Color Nickname
    • Unique rewards like birthday gifts, emotes, and more!
    Additionally, Prime players will gain early access to El Royale events and an additional chance to purchase re-released items.
  • How to increase your Prime Level: Players can increase their level by earning Prime Points (1 Diamond = 1 Prime Point). All previously earned Diamonds will be converted into Prime Points and count toward your Prime Level progress.
  • How to Maintain Your Prime Level: Once you become a Prime player, you'll need to earn any number of Prime Points each month to maintain your current level. Otherwise, on the first day of the following month, your Prime Level will decrease by 1, which will continue monthly until you reach level 1. Earn any number of points to restore your highest previously achieved Prime Level.
